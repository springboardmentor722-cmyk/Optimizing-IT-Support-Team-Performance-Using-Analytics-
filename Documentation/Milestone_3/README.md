# Supportlytics: Performance & Advanced Analysis (Milestone 3)

## 1. Performance Metrics
This milestone shifts the focus from operational volume to operational efficiency. Key performance indicators (KPIs) were established:
- **Average Resolution Time**: The mean duration required to fully resolve a ticket from start to finish.
- **Average First Response Time**: The mean time taken for a support agent to make initial contact with the user after a ticket is filed.

## 2. Analysis
Deeper analytical queries were executed to understand how different variables impact service efficiency:
- **Resolution Time by Channel**: Evaluated whether tickets submitted via Phone are resolved faster than those submitted via Email or Web Portal.
- **Response Time by Priority**: Assessed SLA (Service Level Agreement) compliance by checking if High/Critical tickets appropriately receive significantly lower response times than Low priority ones.
- **Scatter Plot (Response vs. Resolution)**: Mapped individual tickets to identify the correlation between a fast initial response and the ultimate total time to resolution.

## 3. Geographic Analysis
Supportlytics investigated the spatial distribution of IT workloads to assess global/regional operational health:
- **Ticket Distribution by Country**: Mapped the volume of support requests originating from different geographic regions.
- **Performance Differences Across Regions**: Compared the Average Resolution Time and First Response Time between countries to identify potential disparities in regional support staffing or infrastructure.

## 4. Insights
The advanced analysis yielded actionable operational intelligence:
- **Identify Bottlenecks**: The scatter plot and response time analysis highlighted specific areas (e.g., highly technical software bugs) where tickets reliably stall in the "In Progress" status.
- **Identify Slow Channels or Regions**: The analysis successfully pinpointed specific regions experiencing delayed first response times, and highlighted that email channels inherently suffer from slower resolution times compared to live phone support.
